Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do I need to log in every time?

No! After the one-time connection, your access token is securely stored. You never need to log in again.

❓ Is my password stored?

No. We use Google's OAuth2 system - your password is never shared with us. Google handles all authentication.

❓ Can I use multiple Gmail accounts?

Yes! You can connect multiple Gmail accounts and choose which one to send from.

❓ How do I revoke access?

Click "Disconnect" on any account here, or revoke access from your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com/permissions

{% if user.role not in 'admin,director' %}

❓ Why do I see other people's accounts?

For transparency and collaboration. You can only send from your own accounts. Admin/Director can send from any account.

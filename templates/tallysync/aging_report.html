How Aging Is Calculated

Receivables (Who Owes You)

  • Total Billed = Sum of all Sales vouchers for the party
  • Total Received = Sum of all Receipt vouchers for the party
  • Outstanding = Total Billed - Total Received
  • Aging buckets are based on Sales invoice dates
  • Payments applied FIFO (oldest invoices settled first)

Payables (You Owe)

  • Total Purchases = Sum of all Purchase vouchers for the party
  • Total Paid = Sum of all Payment vouchers for the party
  • Outstanding = Total Purchases - Total Paid
  • Aging buckets are based on Purchase invoice dates
  • Payments applied FIFO (oldest bills settled first)
Note: Aging buckets (0-30, 31-60, 61-90, 90+ days) are calculated from today's date. Only parties with positive outstanding balances are shown.
